Banana Bread

Description

Here we have a Banana Bread recipe, supposedly it is the best ever. We will just have to try it out and see!

All of the ingredients are stuff that you pretty much always have in your kitchen - so that is a plus. Except maybe buttermilk. :(

Stuff:

  • 2 medium bowls
  • 9 x 5 x 2 3/4 loaf bread pan
  • Whisk
  • Patience

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup - Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup - Canola Oil
  • 1/3 Cup - Buttermilk (must use)
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla
  • 2 - Eggs (1 egg and 1 egg yolk)
  • 2/3 Cup - Chopped Pecans or Black Walnuts
  • 3 - Mashed Bananas (very ripe)
  • 1 Cup - Flour
  • 1/4 tsp - Salt
  • 3/4 tsp - Baking Soda

Prep:

  1. Combine the dry ingredients flour, salt, baking soda in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Whisk (manually)together sugar, oil, buttermilk, vanilla, eggs in a medium bowl until smooth.
  3. Pour the above wet ingredients over the dry ingredients and whisk until combined. Do not over whisk.
  4. Fold the pecans and bananas into the above mixture.
  5. Pour into greased and floured loaf bread pan

Baking:

  • Pre-heat oven to 350Â°F
  • Bake for 55-60 minutes until golden brown and toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.
