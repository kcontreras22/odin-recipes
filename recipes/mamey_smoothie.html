Mamey Smoothie

Description

This traditional mamey sapote smoothie is a sweet and refreshing tropical drink made with Mamey (Sapote) fruit, milk,vanilla, sugar, and cinnamon. Use a ripe mamey for this smoothie recipe. It should be firm but not hard, and if you scratch off some skin close to the stem it should be a reddish color. If it is green it is not ripe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups - Mamey (cubed)
  • 1 1/2 Cups - Milk
  • 3 tbsp - Sugar (plus more if needed)
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 tsp - Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Cup - Ice

Prep:

  1. Blend on high until ice is blended.
